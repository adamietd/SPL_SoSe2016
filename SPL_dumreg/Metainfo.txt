NAME OF QUANTLET:	SPL_dumreg

PUBLISHED IN:		Statistical Programming Languages

DESCRIPTION:		This quantlet refers to SPL_imp where a data set from a survey is loaded into the program R. 
			Within the quantlet (SPL_dumreg) the relation of a voluntary price and income is observed. 
			Therefor, four dummy variables of income groups are coded to see if the price paid voluntarily
			rises with income.
